The Gay Panic

A brief history of the Gay Panic in the late 1900's, and its relevance to the present day Trans Panic.

(source: Buzzfeed News)

The phrase "Gay Panic" refers to homophobic fearmongering and violence. Gay panic and trans panic have been used as a legal defense for excusing murder and violence against queer people, especially queer people of color.

It wasn't until 1973 that being gay was removed from the DSM as a mental disorder. For trans people to access hormones, a mental health diagnosis of gender dysphoria is still required to make insurers pay for hormones.
